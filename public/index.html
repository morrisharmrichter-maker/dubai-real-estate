Why Dubai Luxury Real Estate in 2026?

Dubai's real estate market combines exceptional returns with strategic tax advantages. The emirate's zero personal income tax policy, coupled with sustained 5.1% GDP growth, creates ideal conditions for wealth preservation and appreciation.

Golden Visa Investment Pathway

Properties valued at €750,000+ qualify investors for UAE Golden Visa residency, providing 10-year renewable residency status with no physical presence requirements. This enables portfolio diversification while maintaining global mobility.
