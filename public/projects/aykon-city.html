Aykon City

Developer DAMAC Properties
Location Sheikh Zayed Road
Launch 2019
Rental Yield 5-7%
Price Range AED 2.5M-10M+

Aykon City is DAMAC's ultra-luxury twin tower development on Sheikh Zayed Road launched in 2019, featuring premium specifications, Versace-inspired interiors, and high-end amenities targeting UHNW buyers and capital preservation investors. This project represents DAMAC's strategic move into ultra-luxury segment competing with Emaar's Downtown positioning, offering Sheikh Zayed Road visibility and premium finishes at pricing 20-30% below Downtown Dubai comparables while maintaining central location advantages and DIFC proximity.

Investment Overview

Twin Towers Development

Aykon City positions DAMAC in ultra-luxury market segment traditionally dominated by Emaar, offering premium specifications and Sheikh Zayed Road prestige at competitive pricing enabling capital preservation strategies and trophy asset appeal for sophisticated investors accepting lower yields (5-7%) for location premium and long-term appreciation potential.

Location & Connectivity

Sheikh Zayed Road positioning provides:

  • DIFC Access: 5 minutes to financial district for corporate tenant capture
  • Downtown Dubai: 10 minutes to Burj Khalifa district
  • Dubai Mall: 12 minutes to premier shopping destination
  • Airport: 15 minutes via Sheikh Zayed Road
  • Highway Visibility: Sheikh Zayed Road exposure creates landmark positioning

Property Types & Pricing

2-3 Bedroom Apartments: AED 2.5M-5M (€625K-€1.25M). Entry-level Aykon positioning with premium finishes. Target yields 6-7% from affluent tenants. Golden Visa eligible with ultra-luxury specifications.

4-5 Bedroom Apartments: AED 5M-8M (€1.25M-€2M). Family luxury with panoramic views. Target yields 5-6% with focus on capital preservation and lifestyle appeal.

Penthouses: AED 8M-10M+ (€2M-€2.5M+). Trophy asset positioning with private pools and sky-high views. Target yields 5-6% with UHNW buyer focus and long-term appreciation emphasis.

Investment Considerations

Strengths

  • Sheikh Zayed Road Prestige: Premier address with highway visibility creates landmark status
  • Ultra-Luxury Specifications: Versace-inspired interiors and premium finishes differentiate from standard DAMAC
  • Central Location: DIFC and Downtown proximity sustains corporate and affluent tenant demand
  • Competitive Pricing: 20-30% below Downtown Dubai ultra-luxury comparables
  • Golden Visa Eligible: All units exceed AED 2M threshold for 10-year residency

Considerations

  • Lower Yields: 5-7% returns modest versus DAMAC's typical 7-9% yield-focused developments
  • DAMAC Brand Perception: Ultra-luxury positioning may face skepticism versus Emaar's established premium reputation
  • Development Timeline: Ongoing construction through 2025 requires patience for delivery
  • Service Charges: Ultra-luxury amenities create elevated annual fees (AED 30-40/sq ft)

Investment Recommendation Profile

Optimal For: Capital preservation investors accepting 5-7% yields for Sheikh Zayed Road positioning; UHNW buyers seeking ultra-luxury at competitive pricing versus Downtown Dubai; DIFC professionals prioritizing 5-minute commute; trophy asset collectors requiring landmark address with highway visibility; Golden Visa seekers targeting premium central location.

Consider Alternatives For: Maximum yields (DAMAC Hills, Business Bay offer 7-9% returns); established ultra-luxury reputation (Emaar's Downtown commands greater prestige); beachfront positioning (Palm Jumeirah/Dubai Marina offer waterfront premium); immediate delivery (Aykon under construction through 2025).
