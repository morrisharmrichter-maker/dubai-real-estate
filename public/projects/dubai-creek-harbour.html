Dubai Creek Harbour is Emaar's 545-hectare waterfront master community along Dubai Creek featuring planned Dubai Creek Tower (world's next tallest building), marina, retail district, and island living with apartments, townhouses, and villas. Launched 2016 with ongoing development through 2035, this project represents Emaar's vision for creating Dubai's next iconic waterfront district combining residential, commercial, hospitality, and leisure components.

Dubai Creek Harbour combines waterfront positioning, Emaar's development expertise, and Dubai Creek Tower landmark potential to create long-term investment opportunity targeting capital appreciation as community matures and infrastructure completes through 2030s.

Property Types & Pricing

Apartments (1-3 BR): AED 1.5M-4M. Target yields 7-8% with creek views and marina access. Golden Visa eligible for 2BR+ units. Suitable for rental income and lifestyle buyers.

Townhouses (3-4 BR): AED 3M-6M. Mid-density housing with creek proximity. Target yields 6-7% from family tenants. Optimal for long-term appreciation as community develops.

Villas (4-6 BR): AED 5M-12M. Waterfront and island positioning. Target yields 5-6% with focus on capital preservation and lifestyle. Premium locations command sustained scarcity value.

Dubai Creek Tower

Planned as world's next tallest building, Dubai Creek Tower serves as architectural landmark and tourism magnet similar to Burj Khalifa's impact on Downtown Dubai. Construction timeline extends through late 2020s with completion creating appreciation catalyst for all Creek Harbour properties through enhanced global brand recognition and tourism appeal.

Investment Considerations

Strengths

  • Waterfront Positioning: Creek and marina access commands sustained premiums and differentiation
  • Emaar Track Record: Developer expertise from Downtown Dubai and Dubai Marina de-risks execution
  • Long-Term Appreciation: Community maturation through 2030s creates multi-decade value growth potential
  • Dubai Creek Tower: Landmark potential enhances global recognition and tourism appeal

Considerations

  • Development Timeline: Multi-decade buildout requires patience for full community maturity and amenity activation
  • Infrastructure Dependency: Value realization tied to Dubai Creek Tower completion and retail district activation
  • Eastern Dubai Location: Further from established employment clusters (DIFC, Marina) versus central Dubai options
